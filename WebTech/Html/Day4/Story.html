🌲 The Honest Woodcutter © StoryLand

 Once upon a time, in a quiet village near the forest, there lived a poor but honest woodcutter named Ramu.

 Every day, he would go to the forest, chop wood, and sell it in the market. One day, while cutting a tree near a river his axe accidentally fell into the water. He panicked.

 He sat by the river and cried loudly prayed silently. Suddenly, a fairy appeared in a flash of light. 🖤 ✨

 Why are you sad ? she asked.

 "My only axe fell into the river. I can't work now."

 The fairy dived into the river and came up with a golden axe.

 Is this yours? she asked.

 No,madam. replied Ramu. The fairy smiled and brought a silver axe.

 Again, he said, No, not mine. Finally, she brought his old iron axe.

 Yes! That's mine! he said joyfully.

 The fairy was impressed by his honesty and gave him all three axes. ✨

 When Ramu told this story to his greedy neighbour, he too went to the river and threw his axe, hoping for gold.

 But when the fairy offered him the golden axe first, he lied and said, Yes! That's mine!

 The fairy rewerded him disappeared with the axe and he lost his tool forever. < sad ending >

🌟 Moral of the Story

  Honesty is always rewarded, and greed leads to loss. ™
